What Ramsay is looking for

  • Commitment
    • In this show Ramsay will endure hotels at their worst, Sacrifcing himself so that unsuspecting guests don't have to. After he has uncovered the most pressured issues at each place, Ramsay will convince hotel owners and employees to work and abide by his standards as he attempts to turn around their failing establishments. Doing all he can to rescue these floundering hotels, Some owners will co-opetrate with Ramsay to address the issues he's recognized. while others will reject to take the advice they once sought out, and face the wrath of Ramsay. Until then, it will be up to the owners and staff to keep their hotels open. Ramsay will look at the decor, the food, and customers, but when the dust settles there is no turning back.
